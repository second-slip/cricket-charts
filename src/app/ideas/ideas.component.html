Ideas

This website is at a very early stage of development. Chart.js is used to create the charts. It is intended to make the charts a lot more interactive.

The plan is to design charts covering cricket past and present. The current charts focus on the Test match career of James Anderson. Here's a few ideas I am working on:

  • a comparison with Brian Statham, the other great post-war Lancashire Test bowler;
  • an analysis of home and away Test performance; and
  • comparison with other bowlers in his era.

Do you have any ideas? I would most welcome your feedback and any ideas for charts.

Layout

I'm experimenting with the layout. These are the options on trial:

  • accordion (the current default design) layout with charts divided into multiple collapsable panels;
  • stepper workflow with charts separated into steps; and the
  • classic design with all charts in a simple series.
